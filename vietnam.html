 The Vietnam War, also known as the Second Indochina War, was a war that took place from Nov 1, 1955 to Apr 30, 1975. There were two belligerent combating sides: the communist North Vietnamese Army and liberation front, also known as Viet Cong, and the southern Republic of Vietnam government.
